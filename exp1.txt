<h1 align="center">COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING VADAKARA</h1>
<p>
College of Engineering Vadakara is formerly known as Co-operative Institute of Technology,Vadakara is the first engineering college under Co-operative academy of professional education (CAPE)established by govt.of kerala.
This was started with a broad vision of assisting the government to impart quality education to the meritorious and economically backward students in the state without much financial burden.
